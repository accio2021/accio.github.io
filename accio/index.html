About Us

Let's start with why we exist. In 2018, the two 13-year-old classmates decided that it's a good idea to team up for an innovation competition. They thought it was a one-time thing, until they tasted the sweetness of victory. They established the first digital maker club in their school with other pioneers. Together, they achieved more than what they could've thought of oin the first place, whether it's some material things like awards and achievement or hard and soft skills that will benefit their whole life. Moreover, they inspire young girls in their school to pursue robotics and programming too. Now that they have parted ways, they decided to build a website that tells their story, hoping that they could light up more people's fire of interest in the field of science and tech.

Project Gallery


Year 2019


Accio



@Young Innovators Challenge 2019
Presented by: Ho Xin Yi, Teoh Yi Huan, Soon Yi Tin

Shopkeepers, have you ever been frustrated by inventory managing? Worry not! Accio is here to save the day.
Accio is a device that helps you to count stock left on the shelf. When the stock count is low, the Accio app will send a notification to notify you. You can also send a message to your supplier to order goods via our app!

Year 2020


Spinector 1.0



@Young Innovators Challenge 2020
Presented by: Teoh Yi Huan, Soon Yi Tin

As one of the co-founder of Accio (Yi Huan) suffers from scoliosis, she turns her hatred towards frequent orthopedic visit into a new innovation.
Introducing Spinector, the digitalized version of the scoliometer (a tool that is used to diagnose scoliosis). With this innovation, patients can track their spine curvature at the comfort of their home. No limitation of location or time. One more hassle eliminated!

Year 2021


Foodie



@Mobile Apps Hackathon 2021
Presented by: Teoh Yi Huan, Soon Yi Tin

To be very honest, we all miss the delicious food cooked by the hawker or famous cooks from that particular restaurant. But due to lockdown caused by the Covid-19 pandemic, restaurants all around Malaysia is banned from operating.
Don’t worry if you still don’t know how to cook! Foodie is an app that compiles easy recipe for people who are cooking novice to cooking. Foodie is ready to help you to enhance your cooking skills!

Foogo



@Young Innovators Challenge 2021
Presented by: Teoh Yi Huan, Soon Yi Tin

More free time during recess? Not a fantasy anymore! With Foogo, you can preorder your food from the canteen to prevent congested traffic at the canteen!

Year 2022


Spinector 2.0



@Sarawak Mobile Apps Hackathon 2022
Presented by: Teoh Yi Huan, Soon Yi Tin

As a continuation of a past year project, we made Spinector into an app! Also, we added some features that make us stand out from the apps available in the market such as:
- setting reminders
- finding specialists nearby.
Now, we can help our rural patients in more aspects!

PosCo



@Young Innovators Challenge 2022
Presented by: Teoh Yi Huan, Soon Yi Tin

Raise your hand if you often find yourself slouching. Whoa! There’s whole lot of people. Alright…alright… we are GUILTY AS CHARGED TOO!!!
However, we successfully found a solution for it!

PosCo is an anti-slouching device that will alert you when you slouch. It definitely is a game changer for people who have bad posture!
